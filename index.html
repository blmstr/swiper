swiper.js

A really lightweight library to make lists swipeable on touch devices (iphone, android).

How?

swiper.js handles the swiping for you. All you have to do is have a list (wrapped inside a div) in your markup like this:

<div class="swipes">
  <ul id="my-list">
    <li><img src="img/1.jpg" alt="" /></li>
    <li><img src="img/2.jpg" alt="" /></li>
    <li><img src="img/3.jpg" alt="" /></li>
  </ul>
</div>

... and then init the swiper: 

new Swiper(document.getElementById("my-list"));
